Catholic.gg Joins Saintcraft

May 29, 2025

We’re excited to officially announce that Catholic.gg has joined the Saintcraft family. Originally launched as an independent faith-based Minecraft project, Catholic.gg had gone quiet in recent years. Rather than see it fade away, we’ve brought it under the Saintcraft banner — and with it, a renewed sense of purpose.

One Roof. One Mission.

This move is about consolidation — not control. By uniting efforts under Saintcraft, we’re able to preserve what Catholic.gg started while giving it the structure and resources needed to thrive again. The spirit, values, and heart remain the same — we’re simply giving it a new home and a clearer direction.

What Changes?

Practically speaking, Catholic.gg content, outreach, and community identity will now live within the Saintcraft ecosystem. You'll see the name honored in legacy content and occasionally referenced in upcoming faith initiatives — but operations and communication will be streamlined through Saintcraft channels.

We're grateful to everyone who supported Catholic.gg over the years. This integration ensures its core mission not only survives, but grows with the strength of a broader, unified community.

A New Chapter for Saintcraft's Vision

May 30, 2025

Saintcraft began as a Minecraft project with a simple mission: build a space where faith and creativity could thrive together. Today, we’re excited to announce a major step forward — the formation of Crosswalk Networks, our new umbrella organization designed to take our mission beyond Minecraft.

More Than Just Minecraft

Crosswalk Networks is about bringing clarity, purpose, and vision to everything we build — whether it’s in-game, on the web, or through new forms of digital community. While Saintcraft will always be a cornerstone of what we do, Crosswalk allows us to grow into something larger and more unified.

What This Means

You’ll start seeing the Crosswalk Networks name across more of our content, partnerships, and tools. Whether it's new server tech, collaborative projects with other faith-based communities, or custom plugins for broader use, our goal is to amplify everything we’ve learned so far — and keep building with excellence.

We're still the same community. The same values. Just with a broader horizon and a clearer sense of what’s next. Thank you for being part of this story — and stay tuned. This is just the beginning.

Ban & Tempban Appeals

Request a fair hearing for your ban or punishment

Believe you've been wrongfully banned or punished?

At Saintcraft, we believe in fairness and justice. Everyone deserves a fair hearing to present their case. If you feel that inappropriate action was taken against your account, we encourage you to appeal and let us review the situation thoroughly.

We believe in second chances and redemption. Even if you did break the rules, we understand that people can change and grow. If you're willing to show us that you want to better yourself and become a positive member of our community, we will hear you out. Everyone deserves an opportunity to demonstrate their commitment to improvement.

How to Submit an Appeal

1

Join Our Discord Server

If you're not already a member, join our Discord community at discord.saintcraft.us. This is where all appeals are handled.

Join Discord
2

Contact Any Staff Member

Reach out to any staff member in any channel on our Discord server. You don't need to wait for a specific channel or specific person - any staff member can help initiate your appeal.

Simply explain that you'd like to appeal your ban or punishment, and they will guide you through the process.

3

Present Your Case

The staff member will create a space for you to present your appeal. Be honest and provide any relevant details about:

  • Why you believe the ban or punishment was wrongful
  • What happened from your perspective
  • Any evidence or context that supports your case
4

Fair Review & Decision

Our staff team will carefully review your appeal, examine the evidence, and evaluate whether appropriate action was taken. We take all appeals seriously and ensure every case receives a thorough and fair hearing.

You'll receive a response once the review is complete, which may include reinstatement, modification of the punishment, or upholding the original decision.

Important Information

  • Be Honest: Provide truthful information in your appeal. Lying or withholding details will hurt your case.
  • Be Respectful: Treat staff members with respect during the appeal process. Hostility or harassment will not help your appeal.
  • Be Patient: Appeals require time to review properly. Staff will respond as soon as they've thoroughly evaluated your case.
  • Accept the Decision: Once a final decision is made, it should be respected. Repeated appeals of the same case may not be reviewed.
  • Learn from It: Whether successful or not, use this as an opportunity to understand our rules better and improve your future conduct.
